About GodLove

GodLove 360 Degree Empowerment Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to fighting hunger, poverty, lack of sustainable income to meet basic needs, by partnering with others who share our goal of poverty free nation.we are a faith-driven non-profit organization committed to empowering underserved Nigerians through skill acquisition, sustainable business opportunities, agricultural training, and essential welfare support. Located at 3rd Avenue, 31 Road, House 15, Festac Town, Lagos, Nigeria, our facility serves as a hub for empowerment, healing, and transformation. We operate as proud affiliates of reputable organizations including TGR Telecom, AFAN (All Farmers Association of Nigeria), and Xtralarge Farms.

Ambition

To establish a food bank and skills acquisition center in Telecom and Urban Farming for 1,000 underserved people in Nigeria yearly

Vision

To be the nation's leading poverty (hunger) alleviation organization that enhances better life.

Mission

Become a front-lined agency for improved standard of living to the underserved Nigerians in these two ways.

  • Repositioning the underserved and low income earners for sustainable income through skills acquisition.
  • Fostering equitable access to food resources throughout the nation

Our primary goal is to reach out to

Extremely indigent families.
Vulnerables and widows.
Elders from age 60 and above
Low income earners.
Out of school children.
Similar organizations involved in the business of eradication of poverty.
