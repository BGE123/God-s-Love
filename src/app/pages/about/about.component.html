About GodLove

GodLove 360 Degree Empowerment Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to fighting hunger, poverty, lack of sustainable income to meet basic needs, by partnering with others who share our goal of poverty free nation.we are a faith-driven non-profit organization committed to empowering underserved Nigerians through skill acquisition, sustainable business opportunities, agricultural training, and essential welfare support. Located at 3rd Avenue, 31 Road, House 15, Festac Town, Lagos, Nigeria, our facility serves as a hub for empowerment, healing, and transformation.

Widows in Farming

Purpose

ALLEVIATING HUNGER & POVERTY committed to fighting food insecurity, hunger, poverty, lack of sustainable income to meet basic needs among the underserved Nigerians (widows).

Ambition

To establish a Food bank and Skills acquisition center in Urban farming for underserved people in Amuwo, Lagos, Nigeria.

Vision

To be the nation's leading poverty (hunger) alleviation organization that enhances better life through skills acquisition in urban farming.

Mission

Become a front-lined agency for improved standard of living to the underserved Nigerians in these two ways.

  • Repositioning the underserved and low income earners for sustainable income through skills acquisition.
  • Fostering equitable access to food resources throughout the nation
