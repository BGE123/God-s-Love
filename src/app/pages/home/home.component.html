About GodLove

Godlove 360 Degree Empowerment Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to combating hunger, poverty, hopelessness and lack of sustainable income. To achieve this, we are establishing a farming education hub and a food bank and also partnering with others who share our goal of a poverty free nation
